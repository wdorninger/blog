GLEIF records may include optional national identifiers if the entity or LOU provided them (look for an "other
identifiers" / national identifier section in the LEI record), but a [SIREN]s not guaranteed or standardized
across all LEIs.

* LEI reference data — full snapshot (complete) containing entity and registration details (formats: CSV, JSON, XML).
* LEI daily/delta files — daily change sets (additions/updates/terminations) to support incremental updates (formats: CSV, JSON, XML).
* LEI relationships data — direct/ultimate ownership and other relationship records (formats: CSV, JSON, XML).
* LOU/issuer metadata — list of LEI issuing organizations and contact/registration metadata.

GLEIF LEI reference records can contain optional national identifiers in the record's entity.otherEntityIdentifiers 
(XML: OtherEntityIdentifiers/OtherEntityIdentifier) section. They are not required or standardized, so presence/format varies by LOU and issuer.

Common national identifiers you may find (examples only)

1. France — [SIREN], SIRET
2. United Kingdom — Companies House number
3. Germany — Handelsregisternummer, HRB, USt\-IdNr (VAT ID)
4. Netherlands — KvK (Kamer van Koophandel number)
5. Switzerland — UID (CHE-*****.****)
6. Denmark — CVR
7. Sweden — Organisationsnummer
8. Norway — Organisasjonsnummer
9. Italy — REA, Company registration number, Partita IVA (VAT), Codice Fiscale
10. Spain — NIF / CIF, Registro Mercantil number
11. India — CIN, PAN, GSTIN
12. China — Unified Social Credit Code (USCC) / 统一社会信用代码
13. Japan — Corporate Number (法人番号)
14. Australia — ABN, ACN, ARBN
15. Brazil — CNPJ
16. Canada — Federal corporation number, Business Number (BN), provincial registry numbers
17. Singapore — UEN
18. Hong Kong — Company Number (Companies Registry)
19. United States — State registration number (varies by state), occasionally EIN (rare/public)
20. Russia — OGRN, INN
21. Mexico — RFC (Registro Federal de Contribuyentes)
22. South Africa — CIPC / company registration number
23. Turkey — MERSIS / tax ID
24. Israel — Company number / VAT number
25. South Korea — Business Registration Number (사업자등록번호)
26. Argentina — CUIT
27. Chile — RUT
28. Colombia — NIT / Cámara de Comercio registration number
29. Poland — KRS, REGON, NIP (tax ID)
30. Czech Republic — IČO
31. Hungary — Cégjegyzékszám (company register number)
32. Portugal — NIPC / company tax ID
33. Greece — GEMI registration number; AFM (tax ID)
34. Finland — Y\-tunnus (business ID)
35. Ireland — CRO number (Companies Registration Office)
36. Luxembourg — RCS number
37. New Zealand — NZBN
38. Saudi Arabia / UAE / other GCC — commercial registration (CR) number; VAT TRN / tax registration numbers
    Other: generic commercial register number, tax id, national company id as provided by issuing LOU/issuer

Notes:
GLEIF stores these in entity.otherEntityIdentifiers[*] with fields like id, type, issuingEntity/issuer or issuingCountry. Match by type/issuer and validate by known patterns (e.g. SIREN = 9 digits, CNPJ = 14 digits).
This list is practical/observational rather than normative; LOUs can supply arbitrary identifier types and labels.
